## What Calculon Does

Calculon is meant for keeping project budgets organized without making the process feel heavier than the work itself. It is especially useful when your spending is tied to a side gig, freelance job, or other small project where clarity matters more than complexity.

The application runs in the browser and supports offline operation, so you can keep working with your budget data even when you are not connected. That makes it a practical fit for users who want a compact workspace for tracking project expenses and watching totals stay readable.
